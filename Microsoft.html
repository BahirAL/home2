The company

Microsoft is an American company heradquartered in Redmond, Washington, which develops, sells, manufactures, and licences computer software. Microsoft’s best known software products are the Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office, Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers. Its flagship hardware products are the Xbox video game consoles and the Microsoft Surface tablet lineup. It was founded by Paul Allen and Bill Gates. Microsoft released the first Xbox series of consoles in 2001. It was very famous at the time, however now, there are many other games, and there are many modified versions of it now.
