Bill Gates

Who he is

Bill Gates, born in Seattle, Washington, U.S.A. on October 28, 1955, is a businessman, an investor, and author, and philanthropist(a person who generously donates money for a good cause). He is one of the most best-known entrepreneurs. Later on in his life, Bill Gates donated large amounts of money to many charities, and scientific research programs through the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. He announced to transition from full-time work in Microsoft to part-time, and changed to full-time work at the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

Early Life

Bill Gates was born in Washington on October 28, 1955, and is the son of William H. Gates Sr. and Mary Maxwell Gates. His father was a lawyer, and his mother was the board of directors for First Interstate BancSystem and the United Way. At 13, he got in the Lakeside School(a private preparatory school). He wrote his first computer program on and implementation of tic-tac-toe that allowed users to play against the computer. Bill Gates and other students found out how to exploit bugs in the operating system to get free computer time. They got caught and were banned. After the ban, the students offered to find bugs on the software in exchange for computer time. Later on, the administrators became aware of his programming abilities, he wrote the school’s computer program to schedule students in classes. He modified it to be placed in classes with interesting girls. He graduated from Lakeside School in 1973 and was a National Merit Scholar. He scored 1590 out of 1600 on the Scholastic Aptitude Tests and got into Harvard College.

Accomplishments

Bill Gates has a great amount of accomplishments. His greatest accomplishment is of course being the co-founder of Microsoft. Microsoft is an American company headquartered in Redmond, Washington, which develops, manufactures, licenses, supports, and sells computer software, consumer electronics and personal computers and services. Microsoft’s best-known software-products are Microsoft Windows, Microsoft Office, Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers. The flagship hardware products are the Xbox video game consoles and the Microsoft Surface. In May 2011, Microsoft acquired Skype for $8.5 billion and bought LinkedIn for $26.2 billion. This makes Bill Gates an even more important person. Another accomplishment is that he was the richest person in the world. This means he has worked really hard to earn a large amount of money.
